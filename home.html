BACKGROUND INFO

Taylor Swift was born December 13th, 1989, in West Reading, Pennsylvania. She has a brother, a mom, and a dad: all of whom support her unconditionally. After moving to Nashville, she pursued country music for about 8 years, then made a big switch to pop. Right now she has multiple homes in Tennessee, New York, and even Rhode Island! She also has three cats named Meredith Grey, Olivia Benson, and Benjamin Button.
